# Discord Greeter Bot

#### 

## Overview



Do you have to do something every morning for your discord server? Any message you want to give someone? Or do you just want someone to know that you see them? In any case, this discord bot is designed so you can automate daily tasks!



Preload a question of the day to send at a specific time, schedule a DM to a friend, or get a surprise with our optional AI integration!



This is a discord bot inspired by a good friend of mine. Hope you enjoy it!
